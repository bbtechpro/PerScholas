Keep Pounding!

The 2003 Carolina Panthers weren't given much of a chance of making it to the Super Bowl. But inspired by a man who had overcome even more significant odds, the Panthers made history. Sam Mills, a linebacker turned assistant coach, poured out his heart to the team prior to start of its playoff run. Two words embodied him and live on. "Keep Pounding."
